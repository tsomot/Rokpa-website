ROKPA         

Tibetans can, Tibetans will

Our Mission

Rokpa empowers Tibetan youth in exile. We work towards bridging the prohibitive gap of resources, networks, and opportunities by equipping them with the tools necessary to make their dreamed ventures a reality. Our initiatives foster welcoming spaces for Tibetan youth to connect with their identity, engage with their community, and ultimately feel supported navigating life in diaspora.
